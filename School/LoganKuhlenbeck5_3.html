Screw Threads Review

1. What geometric shape describes the path of a screw thread?
Helix
2. True or False? Threads have one purpose, which is holding parts together.
False
3. A thread form that has a 7° slope on one side of the ridge and a 45° slope on the other side is the _____ thread form.
Buttress
4. On industrial prints, which is not a way that screw threads usually appear?
True Projection
5. True or False? Inch screw are classified by how precise they are made, with 2 being most common, 1 being less precise, and 3 being more precise.
True
6. A two-start thread is when there are two ridges that form the screw thread, resulting in a(n) _____ equal to twice the pitch.
Lead
7. What term describes threads that are made so that counterclockwise motion advances the thread instead of the normal clockwise motion?
Left-Hand (reverse)
8. True or False? Even if the drawing or print uses decimal numbers throughout, fractions can still be used in thread notes because these sizes are still commonly expressed that way.
True
